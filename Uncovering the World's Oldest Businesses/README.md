# Exploring the World's Oldest Businesses

## Overview

In this project, I analyzed historical business data to explore some of the world's oldest continuously operating companies. By combining business, country, and category datasets, I identified the oldest business on each continent, examined countries with missing business records, and investigated the oldest businesses within different industries across continents.

## Key Insights

* Identified the oldest continuously operating business on each continent.
* Merged multiple datasets containing business, country, and industry information.
* Determined the number of countries on each continent with missing business records.
* Identified the oldest business within each industry category across different continents.

## Skills Demonstrated

* Data manipulation with Pandas
* Merging multiple datasets
* Grouping and aggregation
* Working with historical datasets
