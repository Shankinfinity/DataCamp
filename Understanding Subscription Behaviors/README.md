# Analyzing SaaS Subscription Renewal Trends

## Overview

In this project, I analyzed subscription data for a SaaS company to explore customer renewal patterns across different industries and economic conditions. By combining client information, subscription records, and economic indicators, I examined renewal rates, identified industries with the strongest customer retention, and investigated how inflation levels related to subscription renewals.

## Key Insights

* Calculated the number of clients operating in the **Fintech** and **Crypto** industries.
* Combined client details with subscription records to analyze renewal behavior.
* Compared subscription renewal rates across different industries.
* Identified the industry with the highest subscription renewal rate.
* Merged subscription records with economic indicators to examine renewals under varying inflation conditions.

## Skills Demonstrated

* Data manipulation with Pandas
* Merging multiple datasets
* Grouping and aggregation
* Time-based data merging using `merge_asof`
