# Analyzing the Impact of Handwashing on Maternal Mortality

## Overview

In this project, I analyzed historical hospital records to investigate how the introduction of handwashing affected maternal death rates. Using data from the work of Dr. Ignaz Semmelweis, I calculated mortality proportions, compared trends before and after handwashing was introduced, and used bootstrapping to estimate the average reduction in monthly death rates.

## Key Insights

* Calculated the yearly proportion of deaths for each hospital clinic.
* Identified the year with the highest proportion of maternal deaths.
* Compared monthly death rates before and after the introduction of handwashing in 1847.
* Estimated the average reduction in monthly mortality following the adoption of handwashing.
* Used bootstrapping to calculate a confidence interval for the observed reduction in death rates.

## Skills Demonstrated

* Data analysis with Pandas
* Data visualization with Matplotlib
* Time-series data analysis
* Statistical resampling using bootstrapping
