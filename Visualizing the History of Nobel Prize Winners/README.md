# Exploring Nobel Prize Winners

## Overview

In this project, I analyzed historical Nobel Prize data to explore trends among award recipients. Using Python and Pandas, I examined the most common recipient demographics, investigated changes in gender representation over time, identified the first female Nobel laureate, and explored which individuals have received the Nobel Prize more than once.

## Key Insights

* Identified the most commonly awarded gender and birth country among Nobel Prize winners.
* Determined the decade with the highest proportion of laureates born in the United States.
* Analyzed female representation across Nobel Prize categories by decade.
* Identified the first woman to receive a Nobel Prize and the category in which she was awarded.
* Listed individuals who have received the Nobel Prize more than once.

## Skills Demonstrated

* Data manipulation with Pandas
* Grouping and aggregation
* Creating derived features
* Descriptive statistical analysis
