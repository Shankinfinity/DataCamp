# Analyzing Nike and Adidas Product Data

## Overview

In this project, I analyzed product data from Nike and Adidas by combining information from multiple datasets, including product details, pricing, revenue, and customer reviews. The analysis explored how pricing relates to revenue and examined whether the length of a product description was associated with customer ratings and review activity.

## Key Insights

* Compared the number of products and average revenue across different price categories for Nike and Adidas.
* Examined how product pricing differed between the two brands.
* Investigated the relationship between product description length and customer ratings.
* Analyzed how review counts varied with the amount of product description provided.

## Skills Demonstrated

* Merging multiple datasets with Pandas
* Data grouping and aggregation
* Creating derived features
* Summarizing data to answer business questions
