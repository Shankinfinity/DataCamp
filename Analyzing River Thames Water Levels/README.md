# Analyzing River Thames Water Levels

## Overview

In this project, I analyzed historical water level data from the River Thames to explore patterns in tidal activity over time. Using Python, I separated high and low tides, calculated summary statistics for each, and examined how frequently exceptionally high and low tides occurred each year.

## Key Insights

* Calculated summary statistics for both high and low tide water levels.
* Identified exceptionally high and low tides using the interquartile range (IQR) method.
* Measured the yearly proportion of unusually high tides.
* Measured the yearly proportion of unusually low tides to observe changes over time.

## Skills Demonstrated

* Data manipulation with Pandas
* Working with datetime data
* Grouping and aggregating time-series data
* Detecting outliers using the interquartile range (IQR)
